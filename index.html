Biography

Lidong Bing is the director of the Language Technology Lab at DAMO Academy of Alibaba Group. He received a Ph.D. from The Chinese University of Hong Kong and was a postdoc research fellow at Carnegie Mellon University. His research interests include various low-resource and multilingual NLP problems, large language models and their applications, etc. Currently, he is serving as an Action Editor for Transactions of the Association for Computational Linguistics (TACL) and ACL Rolling Review (ARR), as well as Area Chair for AI conferences and Associate Editor for AI journals.
